IRATUS — Latin for "wrathful." This is Tapp Amps at their most aggressive: a 100-watt all-tube amplifier designed for players who need relentless gain, crushing low end, and razor-sharp articulation.

## Engineered for Heavy

The IRATUS was born from the demands of modern heavy music. Where many high-gain amplifiers sacrifice clarity for saturation, the IRATUS delivers both. Every note in a down-tuned chord rings with definition. Every riff cuts through the densest mix.

The voicing is deliberately modern — tighter and more focused than the Marzian, with an extended low-end response and a midrange character that sits perfectly in heavy productions.

## Unrelenting Power

With 100 watts on tap from a matched EL34 power section, the IRATUS delivers stadium-level volume and authority. The power section is voiced for maximum impact — punchy, fast, and tight, with just enough sag to keep things musical.

## Precision Control

Despite its aggressive nature, the IRATUS offers precise tonal control. Shape your sound from crushing modern metal to classic high-gain rock. The responsive EQ section lets you dial in exactly the tone you need, whether you're tracking in the studio or shaking the walls on stage.
