The Marzian Mini takes the soul of the full-size Marzian and distills it into a more manageable 30-watt platform — perfect for studio work, smaller stages, and players who want to push their amp into sweet spots at reasonable volumes.

## Same DNA, Lower Wattage

Don't let the "Mini" fool you. This amp carries the same tonal DNA as its bigger sibling. The EL34-driven power section delivers the characteristic Tapp Amps voice: tight, articulate, and rich in harmonic overtones.

At 30 watts, the Marzian Mini lets you reach power tube saturation at volumes that won't get you evicted — while still delivering enough headroom and authority for live performance.

## Studio Weapon

In the studio, the Marzian Mini truly shines. Recording engineers love the ability to drive the amp into natural breakup while keeping SPL levels manageable. The result is recorded tones with depth, dimension, and dynamic response that modelers simply can't replicate.

## Versatile & Responsive

From glassy cleans to full-bore distortion, the Marzian Mini responds to your touch and your guitar's volume knob. Roll back for chimey, bell-like cleans. Dig in for thick, singing sustain. This is an amp that rewards expressive players.
