The Marzian is the flagship amplifier from Tapp Amps — a full-power 100-watt all-tube head designed for players who demand uncompromising tone and dynamic response.

Built entirely by hand in Borlänge, Sweden, each Marzian is a labor of precision and passion. From the hand-selected components to the proprietary PCB design, every detail is crafted to deliver an amplifier that breathes, responds, and inspires.

## Tone & Character

The Marzian delivers a wide tonal palette spanning from crystalline cleans to saturated, harmonically rich overdrive. The clean channel offers headroom and clarity that lets your playing and your instrument speak, while the high-gain channel delivers thick, articulate distortion that remains musical at every gain setting.

The EL34 power section provides that unmistakable midrange push and natural compression that has defined the sound of rock and metal for decades — but with the Tapp Amps signature: tight low end, present mids, and articulate highs that cut without harshness.

## Built Without Compromise

Every Marzian is built on request. There is no assembly line, no shortcuts, no mass production. Each amp is individually crafted, tested, and voiced before it leaves the workshop. This is an amplifier built for players who understand that tone is everything.
