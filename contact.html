Ordering Process

All Tapp Amps are built on request. Here's how it works:

  1. Get in touch — Email us or reach out on social media to start the conversation.
  2. Discuss your needs — We'll talk about which model suits your playing and any customization options.
  3. Build & delivery — Your amp is handcrafted in our Borlänge workshop, tested thoroughly, and shipped to you.
