## The Story

Tapp Amps is a boutique amplifier manufacturer based in Borlänge, Sweden. Every amplifier that leaves our workshop is designed, built, and tested by hand — no assembly lines, no mass production, no compromises.

We build tube amplifiers the way they should be built: with carefully selected components, point-to-point wiring, and an obsessive attention to detail that only a small workshop can deliver.

## Philosophy

We believe that a great amplifier is more than a piece of equipment — it's an instrument in itself. It should respond to your playing, reward your dynamics, and inspire you to play better. That's the standard we hold every Tapp Amp to.

Our amplifiers are built on request only. Each unit is made specifically for its owner, with the care and attention that a handcrafted instrument deserves. We don't cut corners, and we don't rush. When your amp is ready, it will be exactly right.

## Made in Sweden

Borlänge sits in the heart of Dalarna, a region known for its craft traditions and its no-nonsense approach to quality. That spirit runs through everything we do. Swedish engineering meets tube amplifier artistry.

## Limited Editions

From time to time, we produce limited edition runs of our amplifiers. These may feature unique cosmetic options, special component selections, or circuit variations. Follow us on social media to stay informed about upcoming limited releases.

## The Workshop

Our workshop in Borlänge is where the magic happens. Every Tapp Amp begins as raw components and transforms through hours of careful assembly, wiring, testing, and voicing into a finished amplifier. We invite interested players to visit — get in touch to arrange a workshop visit.
